Customer feedback from mid-June to late-August 2025 indicates that "Account Linking & Chime Issues" and "Notifications & Support" were the most frequent themes overall, particularly peaking in July. While "Account Linking & Chime Issues" saw a significant increase in July, both this theme and "Advance Application & Criteria" show a noticeable decrease in August compared to the previous month, whereas "Identity & Verification Problems" and "User Interface & Performance" remained relatively stable throughout the period.
